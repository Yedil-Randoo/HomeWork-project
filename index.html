Problem & Solution

Project Description

The Product: A specialized academic record-keeping platform designed for students to organize homework, set deadlines, and manage projects in one clean interface.

The Problem: Traditional electronic diaries like Edupage or Kundelik lack flexibility for personal notes. In a paperless age, students need a centralized digital hub.

Uniqueness: Unlike standard planners, our tool integrates Subject-based organization with deep annotation features, ensuring no task is ever forgotten.

Visuals & Logic

Product Demo

Our system follows a simple logic: Input → Track → Complete. Users interact with a dashboard that provides instant visual feedback on their academic status.

Step-by-step: Open the dashboard, click "Add Assignment", fill in the subject/deadline, and click "Save". Your task is now archived and tracked.
